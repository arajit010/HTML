A currency converter is a financial tool that helps simplify international transactions, travel, and investments. It provides real-time exchange rates, allowing individuals and businesses to convert the value of one currency into another.

Our Objective: 
Develop an application that can covert the value of one currency into another using html, css, js and ExchangeRate-API for currency conversion.

Our appliation can :
Help travelers - 
Currency converters can help travelers understand the price of foreign goods and services. They can also help travelers determine how their own currencies will fare when exchanged with other foreign currency.

Help businesses -
Currency converters can help international import and export businesses determine the selling and buying profits of different products. They can also allow businesses to make worldwide payments on the go.

Help forex traders -
Currency converters can help forex traders achieve speed and accuracy in trades.
